## 1. 🎣 Opening Hook (0:00 - 0:30)

> *"Imagine sitting under a banyan tree in a remote Bengal village. An 82-year-old farmer is telling you how his ancestors predicted monsoons using insect calls and seed-cracking sounds—techniques that survived 300 years of droughts. Now picture this: two years later, that elder passes away. His story was never written down. It is gone forever.*
>
> *Distinguished judges, in India, every time an elder passes away, a library burns to the ground. Today, I present **LokKatha AI**—an AI-powered cultural memory platform built on **Google Gemma 4** that ensures our living history never dies."*

---

## 2. 📖 Storytelling & Problem Statement (0:30 - 1:15)

> *"I am a Computer Science student, but every weekend, I put on my NGO volunteer badge. We organize health drives, blood donation camps, and plastic-free campaigns in rural communities. 
>
> During these events, I spend hours talking to elders, artisans, folk singers, and temple priests. I realized something heartbreaking: India has 1.4 billion people and 1,600 dialects, but 90% of our traditional ecological knowledge, folk medicine, and local history is strictly oral. Their children are moving to cities or glued to smartphones. Nobody is recording them.
>
> Existing archives fail because they are bureaucratic, English-centric, manual, and non-interactive. You cannot ask a standard archive a question in Bengali and get an instant answer."*

---

## 3. 📊 High-Impact Statistics (1:15 - 1:45)

> *"Let us look at the stark numbers:
> - **300+ Million** Indians cannot read or write, making oral history their only medium.
> - **22 Official Languages & 1,600+ Dialects** are facing linguistic erosion.
> - **0 Interactivity:** 99% of existing digitized recordings sit as unindexed audio files on isolated drives, completely unsearchable by citizens or researchers."*

---

## 4. 💡 The Solution: LokKatha AI (1:45 - 2:30)

> *"LokKatha AI transforms unrecorded oral speech into an interactive digital heritage memory.
> Here is how simple it is:
> 1. **Capture:** A field volunteer records an elder's audio story on a low-cost smartphone with digital consent.
> 2. **Transcribe:** OpenAI Whisper transcribes the raw audio, handling background village noise.
> 3. **Understand:** **Google Gemma 4** acts as our cultural intelligence—summarizing the story, extracting cultural tags like *Traditional Ecological Knowledge*, and translating it into **English, Bengali, and Hindi**.
> 4. **Interact:** Citizens, students, and researchers can open LokKatha AI and ask natural language questions in any language, receiving grounded answers backed by direct audio timestamp citations."*

---

## 5. 🧠 Why Gemma 4 is Core to Our System (2:30 - 3:15)

> *"Why did we build this with Google Gemma 4?
> When we benchmarked standard closed LLMs on regional Indic narratives, they repeatedly failed—losing idioms, messing up honorific registers like 'Aap' vs 'Tumi', or echoing Bengali text back untranslated.
>
> **Gemma 4 is fundamentally superior for Indic culture:**
> - Its native training corpus captures Indian language scripts and cultural nuances effortlessly.
> - Its multi-task capabilities allow us to extract summaries, tags, and 3-language translations in a single optimized pass.
> - Its open-weight architecture allows us to deploy lightweight 4B models directly onto offline field laptops for rural NGO workers without internet access!"*

---

## 6. 🖥️ Demo Flow Highlights (3:15 - 4:00)

> *"Let me show you our live MVP demo.
> 1. We upload a field audio recording of a *Purulia Baul folk singer*.
> 2. The pipeline ingests the file, detects Bengali speech, and executes Gemma 4.
> 3. In seconds, Gemma 4 generates a title, narrative summary, cultural tags (*Folk Music, Spiritual Philosophy*), and translations into Hindi and English.
> 4. Now, I ask the system: *'What is the spiritual meaning of the Ektara instrument in Bengali folk songs?'*
> 5. RAG retrieves the relevant narrative chunk from ChromaDB, and Gemma 4 generates an attributed answer with a clickable audio timestamp `[01:42]`!"*
